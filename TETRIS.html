TETRIS, a timeless classic in the world of gaming, is a puzzle video game that challenges players to arrange falling tetrominoes (geometric shapes made up of four square blocks) into complete lines. Deceptively simple yet endlessly engaging, TETRIS has become a cultural phenomenon since its inception, captivating players with its easy-to-learn, difficult-to-master gameplay.

With a history spanning multiple decades, TETRIS remains a foundational title in the puzzle genre. The goal is straightforward: create solid horizontal lines to clear the screen. However, the speed and complexity increase as players progress, making strategic thinking and quick reflexes essential for success. TETRIS's elegant design and addictive nature have solidified its place in gaming history.
