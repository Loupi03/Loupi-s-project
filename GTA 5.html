Grand Theft Auto V (GTA 5) is an open-world action-adventure game developed by Rockstar North. Set in the fictional state of San Andreas, the game follows the lives of three criminals as they plan and execute a series of heists. With its vast and immersive open-world environment, realistic characters, and a gripping narrative, GTA 5 stands as a landmark title in the gaming industry.

  • Game ID: GTA 652
  • Genre: Action, Survival, Adventure

GTA 5 doesn't just offer a compelling single-player experience; it's also renowned for its expansive multiplayer mode, Grand Theft Auto Online. In GTA Online, players can explore the dynamic open world, engage in various activities, and participate in heists with friends, adding an enduring and social aspect to the GTA universe.


AdventureSeeker:

GTA 5 is a true sandbox experience. The ability to switch between characters, the vast open world, and the freedom to wreak havoc or play by the rules create an immersive and diverse gaming experience. Rockstar's attention to detail and ongoing support for GTA Online make it a must-play title.
