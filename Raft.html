Raft

Raft is a survival game that challenges players to navigate an endless ocean on a small raft. The game begins with a lone player stranded on a tiny raft, armed with a hook to gather resources from the surrounding sea. As players expand their raft, they must manage resources, fend off hunger and thirst, and avoid the dangers of the open water. The game's emphasis on creativity and cooperation makes for an engaging and unpredictable survival experience.

  • Game ID: RT 9258
  • Genre: Action, Survival, Adventure
  • Multi-tags: Survival, Battle

Embark on a thrilling oceanic adventure in Raft, where players must utilize their ingenuity to build and expand their raft while exploring the vast, procedurally generated ocean. The game encourages collaboration as players work together to gather resources, battle sharks, and uncover the mysteries hidden beneath the waves. With a unique blend of survival elements and strategic building, Raft delivers an immersive and challenging gameplay experience.
