Dying Light is a 2015 survival horror video game developed by Techland and published by Warner Bros. Interactive Entertainment. The game's story follows an undercover agent named Kyle Crane who is sent to infiltrate a quarantine zone in a fictional Middle Eastern city called Harran.

Great game, definitely better than dying light 2. Dying light has very good gore, there’s lots of guts, dismembered body parts and making heads explode, or just the whole body if you have a DIY grenade. The first game is centred mostly on the zombie part of a ZOMBIE game, while dying light 2 went the other way and made the game a lot more about people instead of zombies, IN A ZOMBIE GAME. The story line of the original dying light was really good, it is both fun in single player and co-op with a max of four players. The campaign has elements of scare, excitement, happiness, rage and a hint of sadness. The game actually makes you care about the character’s unlike quite a lot of games sadly. It’s an open world game so you can free roam the streets in the slums, or the tops of roofs in sector zero. You’re given options as you don’t have to follow the story line if you don’t want to and can do things your in way whether it’s stealth, guns blazing or hiding and camouflaging with dead zombie guts everywhere you go. I forgot to mention one last detail, all the guns, melee weapons and throwables have just delightful descriptions as Kyle Crane (main protagonist) like to crack some jokes throughout the game.


(updated review) This game is one of my all-time favorite zombie games of all time. It does what dead island does but better. This game is really fun with friends and random people. I love the parkour asset where instead of running or driving to point A to point B, you can climb trees, jump overbuilding, and crash through roofs it's so much fun. They are still adding content to this day. The DLC was ok but was still a lot of fun, but it took away some of the parkour assists with driving, but I still recommend getting the DLC. I really love this game and recommend this to anyone who played dead island and wants to play a good zombie game. I can't wait until dying light 2 come out it's going to be such a good game I just feel it.
